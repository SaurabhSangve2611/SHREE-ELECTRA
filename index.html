About Us

We Are Leader In Industrial Market

SHREE ELECTRA was founded in 2016 by a team of ambitious and enterprising professionals with refined skills honed through their experiences with leading destribution and power transformer reparing and AIS, GIS substation maintainance company in India. Incorporated in Maharashtra, India. SHREE ELECTRA serves as a comprehensive provider of Engineering, Procurement, Trading, Switchgear Maintenance, and Electrical Project Construction services across the country. SHREE ELECTRA specializes in a wide range of services, including AIS and GIS substation, transformers installation, testing, commissioning, and Solar Energy solutions, offering customers end-to-end solutions with a single point of responsibility. The company’s key focus areas include Industrial Electrification, Substations, Switchyards, Renewable Energy, HVAC, Cold Storage, Solar Power, and more. SHREE ELECTRA is particularly adept in Gas Insulated Switchgear installation, testing, commissioning, troubleshooting, Partial Discharge monitoring, and refurbishment. Every member of the SHREE ELECTRA team is thoroughly trained and aligned with the values of Health, Safety, and Environmental protection. Continuous learning and on-the-job training ensure the competency of each team member, while the dedicated Testing Engineering team constantly enhances qualitative measures to deliver the best possible outcomes for the company’s projects.
